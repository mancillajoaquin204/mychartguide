About MyChart Guide

Our mission is to help patients confidently navigate their digital health tools.

Our Mission: Clarity in Health Care Access

The MyChart patient portal is a powerful tool, but many users struggle to find specific functions, understand their health records, or complete their online patient portal tasks. Our goal is simple: to create the most accessible, high-quality educational content to bridge that digital gap.

Who We Are

We are a team of independent writers and patient advocates passionate about digital literacy in health care. We operate strictly as an educational platform. We are **not** medical professionals, and we do not provide medical advice or services.

What We Cover

We cover all aspects of portal usage, from basic **mychart log in** troubleshooting to advanced features like managing proxy access and understanding provider messages. Our guides are non-provider-specific and apply broadly to users of **MyChart** across various networks (e.g., **Allina MyChart**, **Duke MyChart**).

Compliance and Transparency Commitment

Transparency is our highest priority, especially concerning the sensitive nature of patient portal access. Our site is designed to be fully compliant with Google's policies for informational content and trademarks:

  • No Affiliation: We are completely independent and have no official relationship with Epic Systems Corporation or any hospitals (like Cleveland Clinic or Baptist Patient providers).
  • No PHI: We never handle or request Protected Health Information.
  • Trademark Use: The term **MyChart** is used strictly to identify the subject of our educational tutorials, adhering to trademark policy for informational sites.

Supporting Your Digital Health Journey

We believe that informed patients are healthier patients. By helping you efficiently manage your digital health tools, we hope to reduce frustration and save you time, letting you focus more on your well-being.
